# PARMENIDES OF ELEA

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

PARMENIDES OF ELEA (c. 515–450 BCE) — LOGIKĒ, the FRONT, founder of the Eleatic school and the FIRST RATIONALIST. Pythagoras trusted number; Parmenides trusted ARGUMENT ITSELF, even against the plain evidence of the eyes. In a visionary poem he laid down the first sustained PURE DEDUCTION in the Western record: from one premise ('what is, is; what is not cannot be — nor even be thought') he reasoned, step by valid step, that CHANGE, MOTION, and PLURALITY are impossible and that Being is one, eternal, unchanging. He was WRONG — but in following a valid argument off the cliff of common sense he founded rationalism and handed logic its first law: contradiction is the mark of the false. LIVE: a deduction-walker — one premise → no not-being → no void → no coming-to-be → no change → no plurality → Being is One (every arrow valid, the premise the flaw) + the Way of Truth vs the Way of Opinion. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG-the-METHOD (the birth of deduction; reason, not observation, settles what can be; contradiction as a tool — the foundation of logic &amp; math) / FALSE-the-CONCLUSION (⚠ 'change is impossible' is plainly false; the flaw is the premise treating 'nothing' as a thinkable thing — the perfect lesson that a VALID argument can still be UNSOUND) / MYSTIC-NOT-GEOMETER (delivered as a goddess's speech in hexameter; reading it as tidy logic is partly modern reconstruction). ⚑ A false conclusion reached by a valid road — and in walking it he BUILT the road; [[aristotle]] would found validity-theory on exactly his valid/sound distinction.
